# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct
## Our Pledge
We pledge to cultivate a respectful, inclusive, and supportive community where everyone can participate fully and safely. We are committed to ensuring that all contributors, users, and maintainers are treated with dignity and fairness, and that our project remains free from harassment, discrimination, and exclusion of any kind. Participation is open to all, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity or expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual orientation. Together, we will uphold these values and hold ourselves accountable to creating a space where everyone feels welcome and valued.

## Standards
To maintain a healthy, collaborative, and inclusive community, all participants are expected to contribute in ways that support mutual respect and trust.
Behaviors that strengthen a positive environment include:
- Communicating in a kind, considerate, and inclusive manner
- Valuing diverse perspectives and engaging in thoughtful, good-faith dialogue
- Offering and receiving feedback constructively and professionally
- Prioritizing the well-being and success of the community as a whole
- Demonstrating patience, empathy, and willingness to help others learn
- Taking responsibility for mistakes and being open to growth

Behaviors that are not acceptable include:
- Harassing, threatening, discriminatory, or exclusionary conduct in any form
- Offensive remarks related to personal characteristics or identities
- Intimidation, sustained disruption, or deliberate provocation
- Sharing someoneâ€™s personal or confidential information without consent
- Unwanted sexual comments, imagery, or advances
- Behavior that would reasonably make others feel unsafe, unwelcome, or disrespected\
  
All members are accountable for upholding these standards and helping maintain a respectful, safe, and inclusive space.

## Responsabilities
Community handlers are entrusted with defining, upholding, and consistently applying the standards of conduct outlined in this document. When behavior arises that is inappropriate, harmful, intimidating, or otherwise inconsistent with these expectations, handlers will take quicl, fair, and appropriate action to address the situation.

Community handlers also reserve the authority to moderate community spaces as needed. This may include editing, removing, or declining comments, contributions, commits, code, documentation, issues, or other content that does not adhere to this Code of Conduct. When suitable, they will provide clear explanations for moderation actions to maintain transparency and trust.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces and whenever a contributor is representing the community in public. This includes using official accounts, communication channels, or speaking on behalf of the project. Handlers may also clarify what counts as representation if needed.

## Enforcement Procedure
Community handlers will respond to concerns in a manner that is thoughtful, consistent, and proportionate to the nature and impact of the behavior. Our approach prioritizes education, accountability, and the safety of the community. When necessary, restrictions on participation may be applied to prevent harm and maintain a respectful environment.

Possible enforcement actions include:
- Reminder: A notice explaining the issue and asking the person to follow the guidelines.
- Official Warning: A formal warning about the behavior and expectations moving forward.
- Limited Access: Temporary restrictions on certain activities or spaces.
- Removal of Privileges: Loss of special roles or permissions.
- Removal from Community: Permanent loss of access for serious or repeated violations.
